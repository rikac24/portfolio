Liger Marine Research Team

Started as a group of eight highly passionate students on marine science, LMRT has now evolved to form its second generation of young scientists who are determined to preserve the dying jewels of Cambodia. Initiated in 2017, these young scuba divers work with MCC or Marine Conservation Cambodia in order to deploy artifical reefs in decimated areas and conduct reef surveys, collecting data on fish and invertebrates populations as well as assessing substrate growth.
